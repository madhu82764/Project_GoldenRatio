Rose decided to visit her granny. She is a mathematician and a big fan of golden ratio so she decides to keep track of the distance she travels and decides to take a break when she covers 0.618 of the total distance. So she's set on for the visit on this beautiful moonlit night. She wants to know the exact distance of her house from the break point. Help her out.

Click on the Start (Start/Stop) button to start the journey

Click on the Reset button to restart the journey

Click on the Stop (Start/Stop) button after starting when she reaches her granny's place( You can decide when you want her to reach her granny's and click on the stop button accordingly).

Click on Golden Distance button to calculate the distance from her house to the place where she took a break from driving (0.618 of the total distance covered).
